## Sign-In

Depending on how you created your account, you can log in with your email + password or with the social sign-in option that you used to create your account.

## Sign-In with SSO

Enterprise customers may have SSO configured. This flow will take a user to their company's Identity Provider (IdP) to complete the login flow. See SSO.
