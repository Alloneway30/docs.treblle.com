## Workspace Options

**Leave** - This is how any user can Leave the Workspace. When you leave a workspace you instantly lose access to everything in the workspace. This means all APIs, requests, endpoints, docs, analytics, etc. The only way back is via an invite from a Workspace Owner.

If the only Owner of a Workspace tries to Leave, they will be prompted to give Ownership to another Workspace Member.

**Delete** - This is how a Workspace Owner (only) can Delete the Workspace. Deleting the Workspace means that all users will be removed and all data about your APIs, such as docs, endpoints, requests, analytics and everything else, will be gone. 

This is a permanent action. We do not keep backups of your deleted APIs.
