## Inviting People to the Workspace

Treble allows Workspace Owners to invite an unlimited people to your Workspace.

To do this, start on the navigation sidebar, go to Settings, then select People. When here, Workspace Owners can enter the email of people they want to add to the Workspace as well as the Role the user should have. See People & Workspace Roles

Workspace Owners can send a single invite or use the + button to add as many email as needed.

## Invited List

Once the `Send Invite` button is selected, the emails will appear in the `Invited` tab of the People list below. You will see the status of their invite.

![](https://firebasestorage.googleapis.com/v0/b/treblle-docs.appspot.com/o/hdg8e_image.png?alt=media&token=03b3fbac-ee58-41f2-abe7-6d291c15ba14)

By clicking in the dropdown to the far right of each name, you can choose to resend the invite or delete the invite.
