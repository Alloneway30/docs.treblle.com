## Workspaces

Workspaces are the highest level organizational concept in Treblle, and are the main collaborative repository for all of your APIs. Workspaces provide a navigable view into all Treblle features including your entire API landscape or subset of APIs.

Within a Workspace, a user or group of users can add their APIs, search across those APIs, manage detailed observability, compliance, and analytical data, get detailed operational and business information about their API consumers, and much more.
